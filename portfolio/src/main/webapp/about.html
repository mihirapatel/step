About

Hi! I'm Mihira and I'm currently a junior at UC Berkeley (Go bears!) studying computer science and data science. Last summer I worked at IAC Applications as a Software Engineering Intern through Girls Who Code. This summer I am working as a STEP Intern at Google.

In my free time, I really enjoy finding new vegetarian recipies to cook/bake, hiking, and spending time with my family. Recently, I've dabbled into design and am doing 100 days of design to learn Adobe Illustrator. Some days I follow Youtube tutorials and other days I design something related to my day. You can check them out on the design page here! I'm also extremely passionate in helping close the gender gap in entreprenuership and technology and giving younger girls access to resources to explore these fields. I co-founded SheEO to inspire high school girls to pursue these fields.
